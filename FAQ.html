Sustainable Development GOALS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q:What are SDG's?

A:SDGs is the common acronym for the Sustainable Development Goals, also referred to as Global Goals for Sustainable Development.

Q:What is Sustainable Development?

A:Sustainable Development is development that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs.

Q:What makes SDG's important?

A:SDGs aim to transform our world. They are a call to action to end poverty and inequality, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy health, justice and prosperity. It is critical that no one is left behind.

What can i do to contribute to SDG's?

A:An individual can contribute to Sustainable Development Goals in so various ways such as by wasting less food, helping people around you, not wasting water, creating new job opportunities for the people, avoiding products that are harmful to the environment, recycling, and buying energy-efficient appliances, etc.
